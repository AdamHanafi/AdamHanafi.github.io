Planning & Advice

Available Services

Your bank accounts, investments, taxes, philanthropy and other finances don’t exist in a vacuum. Our planning and advice services help bring them together into one comprehensive strategy to help you achieve your goals.

Goals-based investing and advice

Our goals-based advice helps you identify your priorities, passions and pain points. Then we help you create a direct link between the outcomes that matter most to you and the wealth management strategies that can achieve them.

Estate planning

Our wealth advisors share their decades of experience and unique perspectives with you and your attorneys to turbocharge your estate planning process.

Creating your wealth transfer strategy

Having a smart charitable giving plan can make a difference in your financial life. Now and for future generations. We provide advice and guidance that balance your good intentions with the benefits of different tax and wealth preservation strategies.

Reviewing your estate plans

When we help you review your estate plan, we do more than just check the boxes. We help you address the important, big-picture questions.

Cross-border wealth advisory

Managing wealth is tough enough. Managing it over multiple countries, time zones and tax codes is even more so. We provide comprehensive wealth management services for those with international lives or families.

Business owner advisory

Owning a business comes with its own set of challenges. That’s why we offer strategic, impartial advice and a sounding board whenever you need it.

Protecting your personal wealth

Is too much of your personal wealth invested in your business? When it comes to diversification, business owners have special needs.

Creating a business succession plan

When making a plan for transitioning your business to the next generation, we can help you ask the right questions—and find the right answers.

Selling your business

Even if selling seems a distant possibility, it’s never too early to start pre-transaction planning. Our advisors know the market inside and out, and are here to help you get the most from your sale.

Executive compensation advisory

Our specialists have thought through the big challenges facing today’s corporate executives—and how best to address them. They can translate these innovative but practical approaches into a strategy just for you.

Optimizing executive compensation

Negotiating your executive compensation package is challenging. We're tapped into best practices and industry expectations. Allow us to help you get the most from this opportunity.

Managing a concentrated stock position

Our advisors have deep experience working with corporate executives to diversify or liquidate their concentrated positions. And like everything we do, we do it with their overall wealth plans in mind.

Planning a C-suite executive transition

Leaving a C-suite position at a public company can have far-reaching impact. Let's put a plan in place that works for you, your family and your company.

Philanthropy

When you’re ready to establish your philanthropic plan, we can help you identify the approach and structure that’s right for you.

Private foundations

To be successful, private foundations, like any business, need smart business strategies. We can help you identify and implement the nonprofit sector's leading best practices.

Charitable trusts

This tax-efficient wealth management tool is a smart way to support the causes you believe in and provide for your family members—during your lifetime and beyond.

Donor-advised funds

Making an impact starts with choosing the right structure for your philanthropic giving. A DAF can be a good alternative to setting up a private foundation.

The Philanthropy Centre

Our experienced philanthropic advisors can help you create a strategic approach for effective charitable giving.

Cybersecurity & fraud awareness

We provide educational programs and materials to help you, your family and your home office staff to understand emerging threats and stay up to date on fraud protection best practices.

Family wealth management

We provide personalized wealth advice, planning and management for families with complex financial needs.

Family office advisory

Our Institutional Wealth Management practice delivers the firm’s robust capabilities to family office executives with the advice, experience, ideas and bespoke service of the Private Bank to support institutional level investing.

Family wealth governance

When a lot of money is at stake, family wealth governance can be critical to family harmony. We can help you put the right policies and procedures in place.

Family wealth succession planning

Transitioning your wealth to the next generation can be complicated. Our Private Bank wealth advisors understand the technical demands and the family dynamics.
